The Earlham Park Hotel is a new independent hotel situated in the western edge of Norwich, close to the hospital and research park A perfect base from which to explore the historic city of Norwich and the surrounding areas. We have 32 rooms in all which are either double or twin, with standard or superior facilities and one suite with ample of ammenities
